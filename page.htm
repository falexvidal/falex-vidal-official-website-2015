Atlantico Stock Photography Sessions

When I was assigned to develop the interface for the new Atlantico's web site, I made some photographic sessions trying to express exactly the experience of working there. In an envirnoment surrounded by technology, we have the human aspect which shapes relations and wellness inside company.

Althought the pics weren's used in the final launching version, I think they could make good photos for stock services. That's why I'm showing them here. What do you think?
